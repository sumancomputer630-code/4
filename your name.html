🌠 Your Name (Kimi no Na wa)

📖 Basic Info

  • Genre: Romance, Drama, Supernatural
  • Director: Makoto Shinkai
  • Studio: CoMix Wave Films
  • Type: Movie
  • Release: 2016

🧠 Story (Simple Explanation)

The story follows Taki Tachibana, a boy living in Tokyo, and Mitsuha Miyamizu, a girl from a rural town.

One day, they mysteriously begin swapping bodies without explanation.

As they live each other’s lives, they form a deep connection despite never meeting in person.

But a shocking truth changes everything, and they must find each other across time and space.

🌌 A beautiful story about love, fate, and connection beyond time.
